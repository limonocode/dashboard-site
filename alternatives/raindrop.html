Raindrop alternatives (2026): the local-first option

Updated July 2026 · Honest comparison — we credit what Raindrop.io does well.

What Raindrop.io does well

  • The most recommended bookmark manager; beautiful previews and collections
  • Generous free tier; 13 years of steady development; 2600+ integrations

Where it falls short

  • Account required; everything lives in their cloud (one-developer operation — a common worry in reviews)
  • The features that matter at scale — full-text search, duplicate finder, permanent copies — are Pro (~$28–38/yr)
  • Bookmarks-focused: tab sessions and social saved posts are out of scope
