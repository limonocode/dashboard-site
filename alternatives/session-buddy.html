Session Buddy alternatives (2026): local-first, but for all your saves

Updated July 2026 · Honest comparison — we credit what Session Buddy does well.

What Session Buddy does well

  • 15-year track record; ~1M users at 4.7★
  • Genuinely local-first: no ads, no tracking, no login
  • Excellent crash recovery with point-in-time restore

Where it falls short

  • Sessions and bookmarks only — saved posts, read-later and tagging live elsewhere
  • Cloud sync still “in the works”; losing a device means losing data unless you export manually
  • Utilitarian UI; a dashboard it is not

Session Buddy vs Dashboard New Tab

Bottom line

We share Session Buddy’s philosophy — your data on your device. Dashboard New Tab extends it from “recover my tabs” to “one home for everything I saved”, on the page you open all day.
